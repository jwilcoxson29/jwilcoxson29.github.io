    Stella Frank

    MA, CRC, CDMS, CCM

    Ms. Frank understands the complexities and challenges of cases that involve a wide range of issues including Worker's Compensation, Short- and Long-Term Disability, reasonable accommodation, personal injury, product liability, marital dissolution, career transitions, and more. She is a Certified Rehabilitation Counselor, Certified Disability Management Specialist and Certified Case Manager. The breadth and depth of her knowledge and experience enable her to offer a creative and unique perspective to each case. She has testified as a vocational expert in more than 8,000 cases. A native of St. Louis, she spent more than 30 years living in the Kansas City metropolitan area. She now resides in the beautiful state of Maine, but she maintains close ties, both professionally and personally, to Missouri and Kansas.

    Kristine Skahan

    MS, CRC, QRP

    Ms. Skahan is an experienced vocational expert and consultant in cases that involve personal injury, marital dissolution, Short- and Long-Term Disability, Social Security Disability, state vocational rehabilitation programs, Worker's Compensation, career transitions, earning capacity assessment, employability and more. She is a Certified Rehabilitation Counselor and Qualified Rehabilitation Professional for Missouri Workers' Compensation cases. Her knowledge of vocational and disability concepts allows her to tailor her approach in a unique way for each case. She has testified as a vocational expert in more than 8,000 cases.
